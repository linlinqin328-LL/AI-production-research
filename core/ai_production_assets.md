# AI Production Assets

## Definition

AI Production Assets refer to structured digital production resources that can be understood, called, reused, and evolved by both humans and AI systems.

---

## Categories

- AI-friendly component libraries
- AI design systems
- Design specifications
- Knowledge assets
- Workflow templates
- Agent capability templates

---

## Characteristics

- Structured
- Reusable
- AI-readable
- System-callable
- Evolvable

---

## Core Idea

Transformation from traditional digital assets → AI-native production assets
